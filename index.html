plus

Stemming from a sudden interest in a certain music group, I began using minus.com more. However having been accustomed to imgur's "download all" button, the new site was very lacking to me.
This script exposes minus' use of JSON in their HTML and downloads an entire gallery of pictures. It uses Python's multiprocessing module (as opposed to threading which is actually slower) and is Windows and *nix safe. The gallery title becomes the directory in which the gallery is downloaded (Unicode supported), all while keeping the integrity of the image ordering of the album. Check it out here →


natural language processing

My interests in natural language processing (NLP) began in the summer of 2011, working with some NLP tools while interning with JPMorgan. The following semester I took an introductory course to AI, CS 3600, where I not only learned more about NLP but eventually led me to starting research under Dr. Ai. My research focuses on the analysis of "hot topics" in social networks using NLP techniques.


strong induction

First time undergraduate CS TAs are required to present an artifact, a project or contribution to the class they are teaching. As I teach a proofs and discrete math course, I created a postage problem generator that tested one's knowledge for completing strong induction proofs.
